      coping guides library

      structured guides to help you manage academic pressure and stress.

      ⏳ time management

      the pomodoro technique: study for 25 minutes, then take a strict 5-minute break. after 4 cycles, take a 15-minute break. this prevents mental fatigue and burnout.

      🛑 panic attack relief

      the 5-4-3-2-1 method: focus on your surroundings. find 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, and 1 you can taste to ground your nervous system.

      💤 sleep hygiene

      screen curfew: stop looking at screens 30 minutes before bed. the blue light suppresses melatonin production, leading to the racing thoughts that keep you awake.

      🤱 student mothers

      micro-tasking: when balancing a child and coursework, don't wait for a 2-hour block. break assignments into 15-minute micro-tasks you can do while the baby naps.
